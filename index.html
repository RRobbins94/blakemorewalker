Design and Project Management

Renovation and New Construction

During the construction period there are many matters to consider including the design process on site as well as the administrative documentation including builder/constructor contracts and insurance.

Members of the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors

A Chartered Surveyor is the description (protected by law in many countries) of Professional Members and Fellows of the RICS entitled to use the designation. We form part of the global professional body promoting and enforcing the highest international standards. The Spanish property system is different from the UK and whether you are are buying, selling or already own a property there will be technical and legal matters that could arise.
